About Us!

CargoCorp is a Gujarat based Logistics Solution company and was the first to load a vessel when Mundra Port came into operation. We provide total logistics solution and work on the goal of client satisfaction and time efficiency.
With the increase in demand and supply chain the need of logistics has increased. CargoCorp with years of experience and great infrastrucure aims to fullfill the demand with primary aim of client satisfaction and timely and secured delivery of cargo.
